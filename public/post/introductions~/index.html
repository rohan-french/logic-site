Introductions

February 27, 2015

* tap tap * Is this thing on? Inspired by Greg’s reboot of his excellent website (consequently.org) I’ve decided that I should probably put together a decent web-presence. Above you can find the usual things one might expect to be able to find on an academic website—most usefully a preprints and links to my various publications.

This is the website of Dr. Rohan French, a Philosophical Logician at large in Europe. I did my PhD in Australia under the inimitable Lloyd Humberstone, and am currently a Postdoctoral researcher in the Department of Theoretical Philosophy at Rijksuniversiteit Groningen working on the Roots of Deduction project. I turn coffee and spicy food into sequent calculi and modal logics, among other things. You can find out more about me here.
